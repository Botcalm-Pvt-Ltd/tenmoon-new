At Ten Moons Coaching and Wellness,

our inspiration is drawn from personal experience.

It is said that it takes ten moons to carry and birth a baby. Each and every one of us also have the ability to carry and birth visions and dreams. This is my bonus daughter Soby, who can attest that rebirthing herself into a place of love, compassion, and healing has transformed her life and al within a ten moon calendar. She is the inspiration and what started the spark to create safe spaces for healing and exponential growth. She has inspired so many people to be their best selves.

When I first decided to foster my now adopted daughter, I had no idea what I was getting into. And as usual for most foster children, my beautiful daughter did not have any idea about her potential either. It took hard work, lots of help and lots of dedication for both of us to see how beautiful life truly can be as a family.

The sad truth is most foster kids come from a place of darkness. And that's not their fault. The circumstances are what makes the mind bloom or wither. So when those dark circumstances change and they are given light, love and care, that transition is hard to grasp. And some may reject that transition, some may try to adapt and some may feel chaos because it's something new and uncomfortable. That's why proper coaching and guidance is very much a necessity. To help release the stress, the relics of habit and to learn that what's important is not what your life was, but what you make of it.

This is our mission

Our Mission

To give every girl the chance to break those cycles of generational trauma. To allow our natural mind and body to heal by giving it the nourishment, the space and most importantly the chance to heal.

I believe that every girl deserves a chance to break cycles of generational trauma through embodied holistic practices. Our bodies have an innate intelligence to heal given the right environment to be heard, to be seen, and to feel a sense of safety. I believe medicating our feelings is a last resort to the path of healing our past.

Guiding the Mind to heal

Mindset Coaching - Through visualization exercises and other methods we guide them to identify their values, to learn working with practical goals by meeting them where they are. Teaching them how to set healthy boundaries and help practice amplifying their voice and needs.

Guiding the body to heall

Healing with Yoga - Through simple somatic breathwork and stretches we work with them to bring in body awareness. Teach them the methods required to interrupt fight, flight, freeze and fawn states. Help improve state of mind with gentle body movements and techniques to coregulate nervous system.

Teaching to keep up

Maintaing Wellness - By guiding to understand the relationship each individual has with food and how it affects them we teach them how to nourish the body with food as medicine. By teaching them mindfulness exercises to tap into the state of mind, awareness and to their feelings, we teach them how to maintain their mental state and health.
